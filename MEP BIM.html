Expert MEP BIM & Coordination Services

We deliver high-precision, clash-freeMEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) BIM services for architects, engineers, and contractors worldwide.

Our Core MEP Services

From initial design to final documentation, we offer a comprehensive suite of BIM services to ensure project success.

MEP BIM Modeling

Creating intelligent 3D models for all MEPF systems including HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, and Fire Protection with specified LOD (100-500) for enhanced visualization and data management.

BIM Coordination

Expert clash detection and resolution services using Navisworks and Revit to create a fully coordinated, clash-free BIM model, minimizing on-site errors and rework.

MEP Shop Drawings

Generating detailed assembly, fabrication, and installation drawings for MEP components, ensuring precision for pre-fabrication and seamless on-site execution.

Scan to BIM

Accurately converting point cloud and laser scan data into detailed, data-rich BIM models for renovation, retrofitting, and as-built documentation projects.

Quantity Take-Offs

Extracting precise material quantities and schedules directly from the BIM model to support accurate cost estimation, bidding, and efficient procurement processes.

As-Built Modeling

Developing precise as-built models after construction completion. We create a comprehensive digital twin for effective facility management, operations, and future planning.

The Advantage of MEP BIM

Leveraging BIM for MEP systems provides tangible benefits throughout the entire project lifecycle.

Reduced Rework & Clashes

Identify and resolve conflicts in the digital environment before construction begins, saving significant time and money.

Improved Cost Estimation

Accurate quantity take-offs generated from the model lead to more reliable budgets and better cost control.

Enhanced Collaboration

A centralized BIM model provides a single source of truth, improving communication between all project stakeholders.

Streamlined Project Schedules

Better planning, coordination, and the potential for pre-fabrication help to shorten project timelines and meet deadlines.
